# Business Plan Generator

## When to use
- Starting a new business initiative
- Preparing for investor meetings
- Annual/quarterly business plan updates
- Pivoting strategy

## Steps

1. **Gather context** — Read existing docs:
   - `docs/strategy/platform-review.md` — Current platform strengths and gaps
   - `docs/strategy/competitor-analysis.md` — Market landscape
   - `docs/strategy/gap-mapping.md` — Implementation priorities

2. **Define the business model**:
   - Revenue streams (Community vs Enterprise vs Support)
   - Pricing tiers and packaging
   - Target market segments (from competitor-analysis.md Tier 1/Tier 2)
   - Customer acquisition channels

3. **Create financial projections**:
   - Year 1-3 revenue forecast
   - Cost structure (dev team, infrastructure, marketing)
   - Break-even analysis
   - Key assumptions and risks

4. **Write the business plan** in `docs/business/business-plan.md`:
   ```
   # Asgard AI Platform — Business Plan
   ## 1. Executive Summary
   ## 2. Problem Statement
   ## 3. Solution — The Asgard Platform
   ## 4. Market Analysis (reference competitor-analysis.md)
   ## 5. Business Model & Revenue Streams
   ## 6. Go-to-Market Strategy
   ## 7. Financial Projections (3-year)
   ## 8. Team & Resources
   ## 9. Milestones & Timeline
   ## 10. Risks & Mitigation
   ```

5. **Review and iterate** — Present to user for feedback before committing.

## Key principles
- Reference existing docs rather than duplicating content
- Use real data from Asgard's actual tech stack and sprint progress
- Be specific about pricing — research competitor pricing in competitor-analysis.md
- All financial numbers should have clear assumptions stated
